About LUNAR

LUNAR is Section #534 of the National Association of Rocketry (NAR). We are about 300 model rocketry enthusiasts of all ages who gather together to learn rocketry, teach rocketry, exchange modeling techniques, and , of course, fly model and high-power rockets.

Launch Information

We continue to conduct launches at Snow Ranch. Launches at Snow Ranch are level 3 with an altitude limit of 15,000 feet and a maximum motor size of M. Prior LUNAR BoD approval needed on all flights above 2560 N.

As always, be sure to check the hotline before coming to be sure there have not been any last minute changes.

If you are planning to launch a High Power Level 1 or Level 2 certification flight, contact the LUNAR Officers mailing list before the launch. If you are planning to Cert Level 3, contact the LUNAR Officers mailing list before you start building the rocket.
